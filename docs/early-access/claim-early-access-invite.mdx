---
title: "How do I signup for the Early Access Program?"
---

If you've received your Early Access invite via e-mail, all you need to do to claim it is click on the `Join our Discord Server` button in your email:

<Frame>
  <img className="w-full" src="/img/claim-early-access.png" />
</Frame>

Once you have access to the Discord server, you'll see instructions that guide you through how to try out the challenges available in Early Access. You can find these instructions in the **#readme** channel.

</Frame>
