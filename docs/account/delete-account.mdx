description: "Instructions on deleting your CodeCrafters account"

To delete your CodeCrafters account and all associated data, please write to us at `hello@codecrafters.io`.

Here's a template you can use:

  **To**: hello@codecrafters.io

**Subject**: Account deletion request

**Body**:

Please delete my CodeCrafters account. My GitHub username is `your_github_username`.

</Card>
